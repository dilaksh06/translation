Call for Translators

We are on a mission to document herbal plants from across the globe and make this knowledge accessible in all major Indian languages, including but not limited to Hindi, Malayalam, Tamil, Kannada, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Punjabi, Gujarati, Sinhalese, and more.

If you are fluent in any of these languages, we invite you to contribute as a translator. You can choose to be credited with your name on our website or receive monetary compensation for your work, but not both.
